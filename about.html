Our Story

AutoCross LLC was built from family values, hands-on experience, and a lifelong passion for automotive performance. What started in driveways and garages grew into a mobile operation trusted for reliability, honesty, and precision.

We work on all makes and models, but our roots run deep in Chevy and LS performance. From daily drivers to high-horsepower builds, every vehicle is treated with the same respect and attention to detail.

AutoCross LLC isn’t just a business — it’s a legacy. Built for the next generation of performance culture.
